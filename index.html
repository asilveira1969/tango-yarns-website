Our Story

At Tango Yarns, we are dedicated to creating artisan yarns of the highest quality. Each skein is 100% natural wool, completely hand-spun and hand-dyed, guaranteeing a unique texture and unrepeatable colors.

Our yarns are designed exclusively for hand knitting, offering knitters and specialty stores a unique experience working with premium materials.

Each product line features a wide range of colors inspired by nature: earth tones, woods, spring and autumn colors that invite warmth and creativity.
